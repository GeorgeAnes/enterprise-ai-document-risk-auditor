# Contract Review Sample

## Service Agreement Extract

The supplier must provide monthly security reports within five business days after the end of each calendar month. Clause 4.2 states that missed reports trigger a corrective action plan within ten business days.

The supplier will maintain 99.5 percent platform availability each calendar month, excluding scheduled maintenance windows listed in Clause 5.1.

The supplier guarantees uninterrupted service without exception. Clause 5.1 allows scheduled maintenance with at least 72 hours of notice.

## Data Protection

Customer data must be encrypted in transit and at rest. Clause 7.3 requires access reviews every quarter and removal of inactive user accounts within 14 days.

The supplier follows robust privacy practices. The contract does not define robust privacy practices or list a specific privacy framework.

## Audit Rights

The customer may request one annual audit report. Additional audit requests require mutual agreement unless a material security incident occurs.

The customer can audit the supplier at any time. This is broader than the annual audit right described in the clause above.
